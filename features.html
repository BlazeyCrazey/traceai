Powerful PCB Design,
Simplified by AI

From AI-assisted routing to one-click manufacturing exports, TraceAI gives you professional-grade tools that accelerate every stage of your workflow.

AI-Powered Design

Let intelligent automation handle the tedious work so you can focus on innovation

Auto-Routing

AI analyzes your netlist and automatically routes traces with optimal path selection, respecting clearance rules, differential pairs, and impedance targets.

Component Suggestions

Describe what you need in plain English and the AI recommends components from our library, complete with datasheets and availability status.

Design Review

The AI reviews your entire board for signal integrity issues, thermal hotspots, and manufacturing pitfalls — then suggests specific fixes.

Circuit Explanations

Select any part of your schematic and ask the AI to explain how it works. Great for learning, onboarding teammates, or documenting designs.

Schematic Editor

Capture your circuit ideas with a fast, intuitive schematic tool

Wire Drawing

Click-to-place wiring with automatic junction detection. Wires snap to pins and route around components for clean, readable schematics.

Symbol Placement

Drag and drop from the component browser or use the quick-search palette. Symbols rotate, mirror, and align with keyboard shortcuts.

Net Labels

Name your nets to connect distant pins without visible wires. Power flags, ground symbols, and bus notation keep large designs manageable.

Schematic-to-PCB Sync

Changes in the schematic propagate to the PCB layout automatically. Bidirectional cross-probing highlights matching components instantly.

PCB Layout Editor

Professional board layout with real-time design rule checking

Multi-Layer Support

Design boards with up to 32 copper layers. Toggle layer visibility, set custom colors, and define stackups for controlled impedance designs.

Trace Routing

Interactive router pushes and shoves existing traces, hugs obstacles, and maintains clearance automatically. Supports differential pair and length matching.

Design Rule Check

Real-time DRC highlights clearance violations, unconnected nets, and manufacturing issues as you route. Fix problems before they reach the fab.

Via Management

Place through-hole, blind, and buried vias. The editor calculates annular rings and checks drill-to-copper clearance against your manufacturer's capabilities.

Component Library

Access a massive parts database without leaving the editor

50K+ Built-in Components

Resistors, capacitors, ICs, connectors, and more — each with verified schematic symbols, PCB footprints, and 3D models ready to use.

Custom Libraries

Build your own component libraries with our footprint wizard. Import KiCad, Eagle, or Altium libraries directly — no manual conversion needed.

Integrated Datasheets

View datasheets, pinouts, and application circuits without switching apps. The AI can summarize key specs and highlight relevant parameters.

Real-Time Collaboration

Work with your team as if you were in the same room

Real-Time Editing

Multiple team members can edit the same board simultaneously. Color-coded cursors show who is working where, and changes merge automatically.

Sharing Controls

Share projects with view-only or edit permissions. Generate public links for design reviews or lock projects to prevent accidental changes.

Version History

Every save creates a snapshot. Compare any two versions side-by-side, see what changed, and restore previous states with a single click.

Export & Manufacturing

Go from finished design to fabricated board with minimal effort

Gerber / Drill Export

Generate industry-standard Gerber RS-274X and Excellon drill files. Preview each layer before exporting to catch issues early.

BOM Generation

Auto-generate a Bill of Materials with part numbers, quantities, and supplier links. Export as CSV, Excel, or share directly with your purchasing team.

One-Click Ordering

Send your design directly to supported manufacturers — JLCPCB, PCBWay, OSH Park, and more. Get instant quotes without leaving TraceAI.
